About The Solblist

Welcome to The Solblist! This list ranks the hardest levels completed by members of Solbris, showcasing incredible skill and dedication.

Ranking System: Levels are ranked based on the AREDL list, perceived difficulty, and community consensus from Solbris players. Completing harder levels earns players more points.

List Points Calculation: List points are awarded using a formula that gives exponentially more points for harder levels. This system rewards players significantly for conquering the toughest challenges. The total possible amount of list points is 150.
